OUR STORY: WHERE WE COME FROM

In 2005, DDR Direct Founder and Chief Visionary Officer Wayne Nugent had a vision of a company that would cultivate the spiritual, emotional, intellectual, physical and financial lives of its members; a company fueled by travel, life-long friendships and unforgettable experiences. For more than 10 years, DDR Direct has been the leading direct seller of vacation club memberships.

DDR Mart Direct is a privately held company with active Independent Representatives and members in 34 markets. Our products and services offer a spectrum of innovative solutions to empower generations and support the potential and promise of our global community.
